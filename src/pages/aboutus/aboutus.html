About Us


Rayn's Ielts Academy is one of the famous english learning institute of Tarn Taran. This institute was started about 7 years ago and nowadays it stands up with its own unique identity in the field of providing education. The institute is famous for the quality of education provided there in and well educated staff members. Institute is providing education to about more than 2000 students per year. Two major courses provided by the institute are English Learning Courses and Computer Learning Courses. In English learning courses one of the leading segment in IELTS, near about 60% of students join institute in order to prepare for IELTS. Institute has its direct links with the Cambridge University (University conducting IELTS) so this is the reason that material they provide to students is totally different as compare to other institutes. Students are prepared on the trending topics as per the guidelines provided by the university and in this way it become very easy for the students to crack the IELTS exam. Institute provides a special class of 9 hours on Wednesday which is also known as the polishing class in which students get number of tips and tricks to crack the IELTS exam easily and to reach their required eligibility level. In the field of Computer education, Institute provides various courses related to computer such as Basics of computers (for beginners), Knowledge of Hardware, Computer concepts, A+, Certificate course in Networking, etc.
